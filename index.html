Frequently Asked Questions

How does HEX generate yield? ▼

HEX generates yield through a smart contract with an annual inflation rate of approximately 3.69%. Since not all holders stake, all inflation rewards are distributed exclusively to stakers, resulting in a significantly high Annual Percentage Yield (APY).

What happens if I withdraw my funds early? ▼

HEX rewards honest behavior. If you exit your stake before the agreed-upon duration, the contract will deduct a portion of your principal and all interest as a penalty. These penalties are then distributed to honest stakers.
